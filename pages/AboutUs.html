About us

Y UMMY TUMMY BAKERY is expanding its reach with the vision to serve bakery food globally. Our research has shown that bakery food is an interesting and affordable food that is enjoyed by a large part of the population, regardless of age. However, bakery food has been identified as a public health threat due to its low hygienic quality.
At YUMMY TUMMY BAKERY, we have developed a unique methodology to prepare bakery snacks in an ultra-hygienic manner while retaining their traditionally yummy taste. We have kept the most popular traditional and famous bakery items and served them in a more hygienic manner for our end customers. In addition, our R&D team has designed and manufactured numerous transformations of bakery items that we are sure will be admired and loved by all age groups..
